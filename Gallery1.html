First Collection of Images


This collection of images features liminal spaces with a moderate amount of context that allows viewers to easily tell where they were likely taken, such as a gas station or a subway. All three images have a concrete and unambiguous setting which makes them more likely to be familiar to the viewer.

All images were sourced from r/LiminalSpace on Reddit. Please visit this subreddit for more liminal space images.
