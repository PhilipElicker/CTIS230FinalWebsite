Third Collection of Images


This collection of images features liminal spaces with virtually no contextual cues or any indicators of where they were taken. Every image in this collection has an ambiguous setting that makes the viewer question where the image was taken, such as either a long term parking garage at night or an underground warehouse.

All images were sourced from r/LiminalSpace on Reddit. Please visit this subreddit for more liminal space images.
